About Us

Built by women,
for women.

We built The Mother Suite because we saw brilliant working mothers doing it all — juggling demanding careers, pregnancies, and newborns — without the right tools to support them.

The maternal care space was either too clinical or too generic. Nothing was designed for the woman who has a standup at 9am and a feeding at 2am. We changed that.

The Mother Suite is a whole-care platform that meets you where you are — whether you're tracking your first trimester, navigating postpartum recovery, or trying to decode a 3am cry before your alarm goes off.

💛
Empathy first
Every feature is designed around real mother experiences, not assumptions.
🔒
Privacy by default
Your health data is yours. We never sell it, share it, or use it against you.
🌿
Science-backed
Every recommendation is grounded in current maternal and pediatric research.
